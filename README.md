# Quantium-Sales-Data
A job simulation by Forage, where I conducted analysis on a client's transaction dataset and customer information, to reveal customer purchasing trends and generate insights for the company. As part of Quantium's retail analytics team, the Category manager for chips required my services to understand who purchases what types of chips and why.



## Data source
This folder contains the data sets used for this job simulation. The "QVL_purchase_behaviour", and the "QVL_transaction_data", were both provided by Quantium for the job, while the "Merged_data" shows most of the changes I made to the datasets provided to make it standardized and easier to work with.

---
## What each dataset includes
- "QVL_purchase_behaviour" contains information on Quantium"s customers and what kind of buyers they are, premium or mainstream or budget, and their family size, which helps us understand how much they are willing to spend to purchase a product.
- "QVL_transaction_data" contains information on the number, price and types of chips sold, and how much revenue is being made from each transaction.
- "Merged_data" is where I join the two data based on the customers' loyalty card number, which appears to be consistent on the two different datasets. This is the data with which all my analysis was derived.
